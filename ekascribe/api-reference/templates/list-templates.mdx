---
title: "List Templates"
openapi: "GET /voice/api/v1/template"
---

List every template available to your workspace: Eka's default templates (e.g. `clinical_notes_template`, `eka_emr_template`, `transcript_template`) plus any custom templates you created.

Each item is the **full template object** — so this is also how you read a single template: filter the response by `id`.

<Tip>
Default templates have `"default": true` and are available to every workspace out of the box. Custom templates you create with [Create Template](/ekascribe/api-reference/templates/create-template) appear alongside them.
</Tip>

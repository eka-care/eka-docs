---
title: Indian Branded Drugs
---

Eka Care has a curated dataset of over 500,000+ branded drugs in India including both non-OTC and OTC drugs. Metadata for these drugs includes their generic composition, form, therapy class, manufacturer etc. This dataset can be accessed through [web APIs](/api-reference/knowledge-base/medications/search) or through our [MCP server](/SDKs/EkaCare-MCP)

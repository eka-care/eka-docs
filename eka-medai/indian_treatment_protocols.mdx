---
title: Indian Treatment Protocols
---

Eka Care has curated a selected set of treatment protocols published by authoritative Indian healthcare institutions such as ICMR and RSSDI. These protocols can provide grounding to SOTA models through RAG applications. These protocols can be access via our [web APIs](/api-reference/knowledge-base/protocols/search) or using our [MCP server](/SDKs/EkaCare-MCP)

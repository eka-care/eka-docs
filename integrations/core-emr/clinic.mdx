## Clinic

A **Clinic** is a healthcare facility where doctors provide medical services. Clinic profiles represent physical or virtual care locations and act as the operational anchor for appointments, doctors, and patient interactions.

Each clinic record includes details such as **name**, **address**, **contact information**, **specialties**, and **operating hours**.

## Notes & Considerations

- **Accurate location data**  
  Ensure address and contact details are kept up to date for patient communication and navigation.

- **Specialty alignment**  
  Clinics may support multiple specialties that influence doctor assignments and appointment types.

- **Operating hours enforcement**  
  Scheduling systems should respect clinic hours to prevent invalid bookings.

- **Virtual clinics**  
  Clinics may represent teleconsultation-only locations with virtual operating models.

- **Scalability**  
  Designed to support single clinics as well as large, multi-location healthcare networks.

## Medical DB

The Medical DB is a standardized medical reference database that provides structured clinical knowledge, including drugs, laboratory tests, symptoms, and diagnosis information. This data enables reliable medical lookups to support prescription creation, clinical decision support, and health record enrichment.

Read more : ([API Reference - Medical DB](/api-reference/medical-db/drugs-and-labs)) section. 

---

## Coverage

The database includes structured information for:

- Drugs  
- Laboratory tests  
- Symptoms  
- Diagnoses  

---

## Use Cases

- Prescription creation and validation  
- Clinical decision support  
- Health record enrichment and normalization  
- Grounding LLM outputs in trusted medical data  

---

## Key Characteristics

- Structured and standardized clinical data  
- Reliable reference-grade medical information  
- Optimized for AI and LLM consumption  
- Informational only; does not replace clinical judgment  

---

## Scope & Limitations

- Reference database only  
- Not intended to provide medical advice  
- Clinical verification required for critical decisions

## Dataset

The Dataset feature lets you connect your own healthcare data to the EkaCare platform. Once attached, agents can search and perform operations on your data — finding the right doctor, facility, or service in response to a user query.

You own and manage this data. EkaCare's agents use it as their working catalogue when serving your users.

There are three types of datasets:

| Dataset | What it contains | Agents use it to |
|---|---|---|
| [Doctor Dataset](/integrations/console/doctor-dataset) | Your list of doctors | Find and recommend doctors based on specialty, name, city, or facility |
| [Facility Dataset](/integrations/console/facility-dataset) | Your hospitals, clinics, and care centres | Help users find a place to visit or filter doctors by location |
| [Service Dataset](/integrations/console/service-dataset) | Your medical services, tests, and procedures | Answer questions about what services are available |

## Facility Dataset

Your list of hospitals, clinics, and care centres. Agents search this when a user is looking for a place to visit or when filtering doctors by location.

---

### What can be searched

- **Query text** — search by facility name, specialty, keywords, or description
- **City** — restrict results to a specific city (required unless a query text is provided)
- **Area** — boost results for facilities in a particular locality or address
- **Facility type** — hard filter to restrict results to a specific category (e.g. hospital, clinic)

---

### Search modes

At least one of **city** or **query text** is required.

- **City provided** — city is a hard filter. The query loosely ranks matching facilities.
- **No city** — the query must fully match (all terms must appear). Use this when looking up a specific facility by name, such as "Apollo Jubilee Hills".

---

### How results are ordered

1. **Relevance** — match quality across facility name, specializations, and about text
2. **Priority** — base priority boosted by any active campaigns
3. **Name** — alphabetical as a final tiebreaker

---

### Campaigns

Campaigns can reorder facilities that already match the query. They cannot inject non-matching facilities into results.

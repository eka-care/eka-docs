## Console

The EkaCare Developer Console is your control plane for building, configuring, and managing AI-powered healthcare agents. From the console you can:

- **Manage agents** — create and configure agents that handle conversations, answer queries, and perform clinical workflows
- **Attach your datasets** — bring your own doctors, facilities, and services so agents can search and operate on your data
- **Configure tools and skills** — extend agents with custom tools, knowledge bases, and integrations
- **Monitor usage** — track conversations, API usage, and performance across your workspace

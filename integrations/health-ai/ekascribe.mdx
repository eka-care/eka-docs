## EkaScribe Use Cases

**EkaScribe APIs** enable healthcare platforms, EMRs, and hospital systems to convert clinical audio into **accurate, structured medical documentation** using medical-grade speech recognition.

With support for **multiple languages**, **specialty-aware templates**, and **flexible integration models**, EkaScribe fits seamlessly into both **real-time** and **asynchronous** clinical workflows.

The following use cases highlight how EkaScribe can be leveraged across healthcare organizations and digital health platforms.
